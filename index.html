  4. Yellowstone National Park, Wyoming

Badlands National Park, South Dakota

First me and my fellow GEOPATHS scholars visited Badlands National Park. Badlands National Park is an American national park located in southwestern South Dakota. The park protects 242,756 acres of sharply eroded buttes and pinnacles, along with the largest undisturbed mixed grass prairie in the United States. More information on Badlands National Park.
